Project Overview

This project involves the design and construction of a four-legged autonomous walking robot inspired by the locomotion of feline bio-inspired robots. The robot has 4 acrylic legs, 2 custom-designed chassises, a spine made of clear vinyl tubing that connects the chassises, 4 HiTec HS-422 servos at each "hip" joint. It is controlled by an arduino, powered by 4 AAA batteries, and has a ping ultra- sonic sensor for collision avoidance. The goal of the project is to achieve a stable walking gait that utilizes the flexible spine.
